## Supermarket Sales Dashboard
## Overview
## This project focuseson analyzing retail sales and data using Microsoft Excel.l performed data cleaning, created Pivot Tables, and designed an interactive dashboard to visualize key business metrics.
## Business questions answered
* **Annual Performance:** What is the yearly trend for Sales and Profit?
* **Product Efficiency:** Which sub-categories are the most profitable?
* **Top Perfmormers:** What are the top 10 products by sales revenue?
* **Customer segments:** Which customer group (Consumer, Corporate, or Home Office) drives the most sales?
## Steps Taken 
**Data Cleaning:** Removed duplicates, handled missing values, and formatted dates.
**Pivot Tables:** Summarized data by year, category, and segment.
**Dashboard:** Built a visual interface with Slicers for Regional and Yearly filtering
